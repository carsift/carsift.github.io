About us

Carsift was set up by William Bicknell and Shaine Ismail to solve the perennial problem of what car should we buy next.
Shaine is an expert in big data and Will is an expert in cars so together built a recommendation engine.

We tested it out on some friends... one never knew BMW did a small 7 seater, another found out a Maserati was in his price range, both went on to buy the car they found in Carsift.
Down to our last £200 we rolled the dice and went to Paris for the BNP Paribas International Hackathon if for nothing else other than a great weekend.

However we came away as winners and a contract to develop Carsift with BNP Paribas to change the way consumers buy cars in France.
